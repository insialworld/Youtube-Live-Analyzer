1. Overview

YouTube Live Analyzer (the “App”) is provided by Insial. We collect minimal data required for operation—mainly authentication details (Google login email), and optional anonymized usage logs (channels analyzed).

2. Data We Collect

  • Google email used for login.
  • Optional logs of analyzed channel IDs.
  • Local app storage used for caching.

3. Storage & Security

We never sell or share your data. Logs may optionally be stored in your own Google Drive when enabled. You may request deletion anytime.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth and YouTube Data API. All data fetched is publicly available.
